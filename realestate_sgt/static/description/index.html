Odoo Property Rental, Sale and Lease Management App

Property lease and rental management app is complete solution all kind of propery and housing management system. you can create manage , renew ,cancel and terminate contract for lease and selling of property-house. based on contract invoice will be created for every month/year according to the contract duration. this odoo apps allow partial payment for property selling.Have payment with multiple cheques. once invoice is created invoice due date automatically set based on interval set on contract. also this app has feature to maintain property maintenance and user commission calculate at register payment time base on property. commission and commission sheets are automatically generated based on the invoice payment. this app also support to view and print contract expired report, property analysis report.
Various reminder mail notification options, apply sale offer and rent offer, apply delay invoice/instalment payment fine, property quick search options by name, price range, type, status, category. easy to attached property documents and images.
